Tim Fisher - Compuware


Tim is a passionate technologist with over 19 years of software development experience in roles including Chief Architect, Developer, and Development Team Lead. He has a great deal of experience in the areas of web application development, Ruby/Rails, Java/J2EE, JavaScript, web application performance, open source software, architecture, design, and development. He is a passionate advocate and believer in Agile methodologies. Tim also has expertise in the areas of collaboration, knowledge sharing, and Enterprise 2.0 technologies and culture. Tim’s wide breadth of knowledge of both current and emerging technologies allows him to often identify innovative technical solutions to customer problems. Tim also enjoys technical writing. He is the author of the Java Phrasebook and the Ruby on Rails Bible. He is currently working on a book on Social Media Development. Tim's main areas of interest include web development, agile development methodologies, test-driven development, rich client front-end development, rich internet applications (RIA), Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Java, J2EE, JavaScript, JavaScript toolkits, web application performance, lightweight frameworks, open source, collaboration, knowledge sharing, Enterprise 2.0 and Web 2.0 technologies.
