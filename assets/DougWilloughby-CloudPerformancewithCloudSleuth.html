Cloud Performance with CloudSleuth

Like the cloud itself, CloudSleuth is a community that extends beyond the borders of any single organization. This community of cloud-based companies and experts provides information, resources and hands-on solutions regarding the best practices, blueprints and tools for the cloud. Discover the reality — and the practicality — of deploying and managing borderless applications to your business’s best advantage.

Doug Willoughby is the Director of Cloud Computing for Compuware. Prior to Compuware he was at Sun, which he joined in 1988 and where he participated in the development and marketing of some of Sun's most pioneering and disruptive technologies, including Project Spring, Distribute Objects Everywhere (DOE), NextStep/OpenStep, and Java. Willoughby was also part of the team of 14 engineers and architects who developed "network.com" - Sun's first utility computing offering.
